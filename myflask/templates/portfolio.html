About Me

I am a highly motivated computer science graduate student currently pursuing my Master of Science in Computer Science at the University of Texas at Arlington. I also have a significant 3-YOE experience as Senior Software Engineer with a strong background in computer science. My journey in the tech industry has been marked by notable achievements and impactful contributions.

My career took off at Brillio, where I served as a Senior Software Engineer from September 2020 to August 2023. During this time, I had the opportunity to work on a crucial Cell Site Management project for Verizon as a Full Stack Java Developer. I am proud to say that my dedication and exceptional work were acknowledged with prestigious awards, including Employee of The Quarter and Employee of The Month.

Prior to my role at Brillio, I gained valuable experience as a Backend Engineer Intern at the National Institute of Technology. This internship provided me with hands-on experience in cutting-edge technology.

I love automating mundane tasks and dockerizing them for deployment.
